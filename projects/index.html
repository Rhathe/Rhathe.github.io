David Robowie

Hey, it's a robot that plays piano! It was my senior project back in college. Essentially, a robot is assembled atop a toy racecar with a camera pointing down onto a piano keyboard and two robotic fingers positioned over the keys. It used OpenCV to determine where and what the keys were, and an Arduino coontrolled the motors and servos to play the keys. All in all pretty dumb for a senior project. Hey, still graduated though. Yeah but still dumb.
